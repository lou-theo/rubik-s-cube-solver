Rubik's Cube and Mèffert's Pyraminx Icons
by Rich D. in 2003
rich@richd.com

These icons were drawn using Adobe Photoshop 7 and Macromedia Freehand 10, on Mac OS X 10.2.4. 

Rubik's Cube was invented by Ernö Rubik in 1974.
The Pyraminx was invented by Uwe Mèffert around 1971. 

Redistribution of this icon package is free and encouraged, provided the icons aren't separated from this Read Me file.
